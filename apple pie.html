Apple Pie

Description

An apple pie is a fruit pie in which the principal filling ingredient is apples.

It is generally double-crusted, with pastry both above and below the filling; the upper crust may be solid or latticed (woven of crosswise strips). Apple pie is an unofficial symbol of the United States and one of its signature comfort foods.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Butter and flour
  • Sugars
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Make the filling
  2. Assemble the pie
  3. Bake the pie
